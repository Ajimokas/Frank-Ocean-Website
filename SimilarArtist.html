Emanuel

If you’re into Frank’s ambient sounds and heart quenching lyrics, I have got the perfect individual for you. Emanuel is from Toronto, Canada and his music is filled with soulful and ambient textures reminiscent of Frank. His song subject matter revolves around unrequited love and hearbreak, something Frank’s music is familiar with. He is a great artist to get into if you love Frank’s music. I personally recommend Alt Therapy, his album released in 2019.

Daniel Caesar

Hailing from Toronto as well is Daniel Caesar! His music contains the lush audio textures of Frank’s music. The subject matter is also similar to that of Frank, If you enjoy music about unrequited love. If you’re looking for a more gsopel sounding album, I’d recommend Freudian, but something more recent and also good is Case Study 01
